Quick guide

How to play

  • Click the glowing collector to gather Energy.
  • Buy upgrades to improve clicks, automatic income, multipliers, and lucky bonuses.
  • Finish quests, daily tasks, limited-time event tasks, and claim the daily reward for bonus Energy.
  • Milestones unlock permanent production boosts and collectible items.
  • Use Prestige / Rebirth when ready to reset a run for permanent points, bonuses, cosmetics, and special collectibles.
